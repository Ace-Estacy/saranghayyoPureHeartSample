ABOUT US

🌟 Welcome to Saranghayyo PureHeart, a beacon of hope in our local community. We are a grassroots local charity with deep community roots, driven by a profound commitment to making a positive impact in our neighborhood. Our journey is marked by 10 dedicated years of service and the resounding success of 5 heartfelt fundraisers.

💪 At Saranghayyo PureHeart, we firmly believe that the strength of a community lies in its ability to uplift and support one another. Our passionate team works tirelessly, day in and day out, to make this belief a reality. We are on a mission to support those in need, to foster meaningful connections, and to build a stronger, more caring community.

🌍 Our unwavering dedication to our cause extends to every corner of our neighborhood, and we take pride in the knowledge that we are part of something bigger than ourselves. Together, we strive to be the change we wish to see, one act of kindness at a time.

🤝 Join us in our endeavor to create a brighter, more compassionate future for our community. With Saranghayyo PureHeart, together we can make a difference that truly matters. 💖🏡🤗🌍💪🌟
